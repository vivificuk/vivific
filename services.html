Services

We can step into a programme at different stages – from early discovery to in-flight delivery. Our services are modular so you can pick what you need.

Architecture Discovery & Strategy

Understand where you are today, where you want to be, and how to get there without freezing delivery.

  • Current-state assessments and gap analysis
  • Architecture vision and principles
  • Target state options and recommendations

Solution Architecture & Design

End-to-end solution designs for new systems, migrations, and major changes – aligned with existing standards and governance.

  • Solution options and trade-off analysis
  • C4 diagrams, integration contracts, data flows
  • Security, resilience, and observability patterns

Cloud & Platform Consulting

Support for building or evolving cloud platforms based on AWS, Azure, containers, and managed services.

  • Landing zones, multi-account structures
  • API gateways, messaging, and event streams
  • Data platform and analytics integration

Integration & Data Engineering

Design and implementation guidance for integration layers and data feeds that other teams can actually own.

  • Event-driven integration with Kafka and queues
  • Operational data stores and staging schemas
  • Downstream data sharing and reporting feeds

Programme & Delivery Support

We partner with delivery teams, vendors, and internal architects to keep large programmes coherent and moving.

  • Architecture runway and backlog support
  • Design reviews, approvals, and guardrails
  • Vendor solution validation and RFP input

Lightweight Advisory Retainers

Ongoing access to architectural support without needing a full-time hire.

  • Regular check-ins and design clinics
  • Review of key decisions and documents
  • Slack/Teams-style “ask us anything” support
