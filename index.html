Approach

Calm, structured, and delivery-focused. The aim is to simplify, not add complexity.

  1. 01 · Understand the context
    Start with goals, constraints, existing systems, and the people involved. Architecture work is only useful when it’s grounded in reality.
  2. 02 · Shape clear options

  3. 03 · Support delivery
    Work alongside teams to refine designs, unblock decisions, and keep the architecture aligned with what’s actually being built.
